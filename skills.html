My UX Skills

User Research

My approach to user Research starts with a Proto Persona. From there I devise a Research Plan to conduct User Interviews in person or through WebEx in order to discover a User’s goals and pain points. In addition, a Competitor Analysis is conducted on at least 2 direct and indirect competitors.
